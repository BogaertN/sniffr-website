About Sniffer

The clear, emotional, easy-to-understand vision.

Sniffer is not just an app — it’s a way of living with your dog. It’s where adventure meets loyalty. Where real training meets real rewards. Where memory, healing, and life milestones are tracked forever.

Built for families, explorers, dreamers, and anyone who believes dogs aren’t pets — they’re family. Every feature, every moment, every step is designed to strengthen the bond between you and your dog, and to honor that bond forever.

Explore the world together. Train together. Heal together. Grow together. Sniffer is the platform your dog always deserved.

Adventure Mode — The Heartbeat of Daily Life

Explore new places, walk, run, conquer training missions, play games like Zombie Chase and Scent Treasure Hunts. Earn XP, unlock Badges, achieve Milestones, and uncover Secret Gifts — all while building a legendary bond.

Sniffer Adventure Mode = Fitness Tracker + Gamified Fun + Trust Building + Memories That Never Fade.

Turr Training Mode — Real Training Made Easy

With the help of Eddie and Penny, Sniffer makes training fun, achievable, and empowering. Start from basic commands like "sit" and "stay", then move into advanced scent work, obstacle courses, and trick languages.

Choose free training packs, unlock secret skills with XP, or upgrade into Star Wars, Lord of the Rings, Elvish, Gaelic, German, and more. Training isn’t a chore here — it’s a journey of loyalty, trust, and fun.

Death 3 — Vet Portal

Sniffer simplifies veterinary healthcare forever. Owners can track vet visits, get reminders, store health updates, manage prescriptions, and create direct emergency contacts.

Vets can log records easily and accept payments via Sniffer, Cash App, Venmo, or Stripe — helping families stay on top of their dog’s health without stress or confusion.

Focus: Make healthcare simple, strong, and connected for every dog family.

Debt Bass — Groomer Portal

Groomers can now connect directly with owners. Book appointments, log grooming sessions, send reminders for nail trims, haircuts, and spa days.

Just like the Vet Portal, groomers can accept payments through Sniffer — making care seamless, easy, and always focused on keeping pups looking and feeling their best.

Focus: Keep pups happy, healthy, and looking badass — always.

Adventure Feed — Your Dog’s Social World

Share videos, XP adventures, funny moments, and emotional bonds. Trainers and groomers post helpful tips too. Reactions are fun: like with a paw 🐾, laugh with Eddie face 😂, dislike (rarely) with a poop emoji 💩.

Cross-post to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook — but keep Sniffer positive, pack-centered, and drama-free.

Focus: Build a real full-pack family online.

Rainbow Bridge Mode — Healing and Eternal Love

When the journey must end, Sniffer’s Rainbow Bridge Mode honors your dog’s life: Memory albums auto-generate, full of your adventures.

Families can post goodbye packs, send love, and keep their best friends’ memories alive forever — with no paywalls, no ads, and no limits.

Focus: Heart. Healing. Eternal Connection.

Recovery Tracker — Stronger Together

After surgeries, sickness, or injury, monitor your dog’s recovery with daily health logs, vet instructions, and live alert systems if something looks wrong.

Focus: Keep healing strong, track real recovery, prevent emergencies, stay connected with your vet or trainer team.
